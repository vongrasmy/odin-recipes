Chicken Katsu

Description

A savory, juicy fried chicken breast that can be eaten plain, or paired with japanese curry.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinnless, boneless chicken breast halves.
  • Salt, pepper, and any seasonings you like to add to flour
  • Flour (enough to coat chicken breast)
  • Egg (enough to coat chicken breast)
  • Pank (enough to coat chicken breast)
  • Oil (neutral oil such as canola or vegatable)

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko.
  3. Heat oil on medium-high. Test oil temperature by sprinkling a small amount of flour in the oil.If the flour bubbles the oil is read for frying.
  4. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
